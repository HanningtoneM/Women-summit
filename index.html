Main Programs

legal systems

The current legal systems are inadequate in ensuring the African woman's complete protection and socio-economic protection.

Gender Based Violence

Women are in constant fear of experiencing gender-based violence and sexual harassment but often do.

Harmful cultural practices

These outdated practices include child marriage, wife inheritance, female genital mutilation, and patriarchal systems that uphold the subjugation of women.

Economic exclusion

Women in Africa remain excluded from economic access to economic capital such as employment with an even higher disparity when compared to women in rural areas.

Inequality

Gender inequality was ingrained by the patriarchal ordering of society, under which political, economic, legal, and social standards are either set by or fixed in the interests of men.
